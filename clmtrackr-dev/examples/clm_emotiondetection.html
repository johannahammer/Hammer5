About

SCHEMATA is a typeface that stands in direct dialogue with the reader and their emotional responses. As the typeface in itself deals with the boundaries of definition, choice and quantification of how a typeface should look like, SCHEMATA acknowledges the multiplicity of states, contrasting conventional use of typography, where an authority gets to choose how matter should be displayed and in what tone.
